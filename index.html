Why is it important that

You Stay Home?

  • 01 The fewer people you’re around, the lower your chance of infection.

  • 02 The main way COVID-19 spreads is from person-to-person contact. “Contact” is more than touching.

  • 03 When someone coughs or sneezes near you, droplets from their nose and mouth go into the air. Droplets from a person with COVID-19 have the virus in them.

  • 04 To protect yourself, you should practice social distancing. This includes staying at least 6 feet away from other people.
